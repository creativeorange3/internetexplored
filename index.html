Why Internet Explored?

Internet Explored is an ode to our old internet friend who's been with us for 27 long years. It was not the best, but it was the worst browser we had. In the end, internet can't stop - won't stop. Internet Explored is jumping in by building various nostalgic utilities (see the about folder).
